The tinmetric-* web fonts in this directory are the Liberation fonts
(Liberation Serif / Liberation Sans), converted to WOFF2.

Liberation™ fonts are © Red Hat, Inc., licensed under the
SIL Open Font License, Version 1.1 (https://scripts.sil.org/OFL).

They are bundled here as metric-compatible stand-ins for Times New Roman
and Arial so that UDF pagination and line breaking are identical for every
viewer regardless of the fonts installed on the device.
